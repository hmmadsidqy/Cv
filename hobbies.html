1. Gaming & Warhammer Miniatures

  • Cavill is a big gamer, especially strategy titles like the Total War series.
  • He’s deeply passionate about Warhammer (especially Warhammer 40,000). He has two sides to this hobby: the model-building and painting of miniatures, and then using them to play tabletop battles with little armies.
  • He’s visited the Warhammer headquarters, collected lore, and talks about how immersive the Warhammer universe is.

2. Reading (Fantasy & Sci-Fi)

  • Cavill loves reading fantasy and sci-fi. Some of his favorite authors include Raymond E. Feist, David Gemmell, Brandon Sanderson, and Robert Jordan.
  • He’s very loyal to source material — in interviews he talks about how much he enjoys the world-building, lore, and detail in these books.
  • He even called Brandon Sanderson to express interest in playing Kaladin from The Stormlight Archive.

3. Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

  • Cavill practices Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ) and trains under Roger Gracie, a very respected BJJ master.
  • For him, BJJ is mentally intense — he calls it a “thinking sport” because you constantly have to strategize, anticipate, and adapt.
  • It’s also a meditative escape; in his own words, when he’s on the mat, he has to focus fully or risk getting submitted.

4. Rugby

  • Cavill played rugby in his youth and maintains a fondness for the sport.
  • He supports Jersey Rugby Club.
  • His rugby background is part of his disciplined, physical upbringing — it helped build teamwork and resilience.
