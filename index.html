HENRY CAVIL

British actor; best known for playing Superman, celebrated for his charisma, dedication, and iconic on-screen presence.

Henry William Dalgliesh Cavill (born May 5, 1983, in Jersey) is a British actor known for his breakout role as Superman in Man of Steel (2013) and other DC films, as well as starring in The Witcher on Netflix, and action movies like Mission: Impossible – Fallout; raised in a large family, he attended Stowe School, began acting in his early 20s, and is also a passionate gamer.
